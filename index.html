About Dr. Leah Miller

Dedicated to Your Health & Wellbeing

Dr. Leah Miller founded Rhea Health with a vision to provide personalized, compassionate care to women throughout all stages of life. With over 10 years of medical practice in Missoula, Dr. Miller has built lasting relationships with her patients based on trust, respect, and clinical excellence.

At Rhea Health, we believe that quality healthcare goes beyond medical expertise. We take the time to listen, understand your concerns, and partner with you to make informed decisions about your health.

Our Philosophy: Every woman deserves personalized care that honors her unique needs, values, and life circumstances. We're here to support you with expertise, empathy, and unwavering respect.
